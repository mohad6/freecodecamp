Dog Breeds

Dogs come in hundreds of breeds, categorized into different groups based on their traits and purposes:

  • Working dogs (e.g., Siberian Husky, Doberman Pinscher) help with tasks like guarding, pulling sleds, and rescue missions.
  • Sporting dogs (e.g., Golden Retriever, Labrador Retriever) excel at hunting and retrieving.
  • Toy breeds (e.g., Chihuahua, Pomeranian) are small and mostly companions.
  • Terriers (e.g., Jack Russell Terrier, Scottish Terrier) are energetic and great at pest control.
  • Hound dogs (e.g., Beagle, Greyhound) have strong instincts for tracking and chasing prey.
